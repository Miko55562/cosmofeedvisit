ABOUT COMPANY


Limited Liability Company "Trading company "Econaturprodukt" is a supplier of products Biryulevsky Experimental Plant (BEP),
specializing in the production of special-purpose products.

The main vector of the plant's activity is the creation of food products for representatives of space departments and for spacecraft crews,
as well as rescue floating facilities, emergency situations and the Ministry of Emergency Situations. The advantage of products manufactured
at the BEP is its exceptional quality. Since the factory is a supplier for cosmonauts and government agencies, the manufactured products are
made on the basis of natural raw materials without adding any dyes, artificial thickeners, stabilizers and preservatives.
